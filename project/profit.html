General Profits

The Edmonton Oilers have a net worth of 3.2 billion U.S dollars (4.4 billion CAD). The team's revenue coming in at 431 million U.S Dollars (592 million CAD), and the annual income for the team was 244 million US dollars (335 million CAD), makes the Edmonton Oilers the most profitable team in the NHL in 2025-2026.
